hotspring® Coil Heaters

hotset was already in 1980 one idea ahead as they presented the first round hotspring coil heater. Since then further innovations have been made: hotspring®/Mini (type WRP 1,3 x 2,3), flat and square, hotspring®/Maxi (Type WRP 4,8 x 8,6) as well as hotspring®/Micro (Type WRP 1,3 x 2,3). This innovation as well as the humidity-resistant coil heaters according to I.P. 65 (type WRP/F 2.2 x 4.2 / WRP/Q 3.0 x 3.0) emphasize the consequent alignment to new challenges.
