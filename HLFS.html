HLF/S

Elstein HLF/S panel heaters are ceramic infrared heaters, which can be used for operating temperatures up to 750 °C and surface ratings up to 64 kW/ m². HLF/S heaters are available in a square frame size, as an equipment for larger heating panels, which can optionally be equipped with or without an additional solid insulation of the heating panel. The HLF/S heater, with it's increased back side, like a pyramid and it's extended thermal insulated internal space, sets a relevant heat resistance and thus allows the target increase of the efficiency of the heater and of the heating panel. Elstein HLF/S panel heaters cover the power range from 250 W to 1000 W.
